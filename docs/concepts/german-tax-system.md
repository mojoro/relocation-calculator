# German Tax System

German income tax (Einkommensteuer) is levied progressively across four zones: 0% up to the basic allowance (€12,348 in 2026), 14–42% in two linear-progression zones, and 45% at the top rate. On top of income tax: Solidaritätszuschlag (5.5% of income tax, mostly abolished for middle incomes since 2021), Kirchensteuer (9% of income tax in Berlin, only if registered with a church), and Sozialversicherungsbeiträge (health, pension, unemployment, and nursing care insurance — split equally between employer and employee). Tax class (Steuerklasse I–VI) affects monthly withholding but not the final annual tax bill.
