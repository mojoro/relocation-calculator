Social Insurance (Sozialversicherung)

Health Insurance (Krankenversicherung)Mandatory public health insurance. The base rate is 14.6% split equally between employer and employee (7.3% each), plus a supplementary contribution (Zusatzbeitrag) that varies by insurer — the average is ~1.7% (0.85% employee share). {{ result().healthInsurance | currency: 'EUR' : 'symbol' : '1.2-2' : 'de' }}
Pension Insurance (Rentenversicherung)Germany's pay-as-you-go public pension system. The total rate is 18.6%, split equally — you pay 9.3%, your employer pays 9.3%. Contributions are capped at the Beitragsbemessungsgrenze (€101.400/year in 2026). {{ result().pensionInsurance | currency: 'EUR' : 'symbol' : '1.2-2' : 'de' }}
Unemployment Insurance (Arbeitslosenversicherung)Covers you if you lose your job — you receive ~60% of your net salary (67% with children) for up to 12 months. The rate is 2.6% total, split equally at 1.3% each. {{ result().unemploymentInsurance | currency: 'EUR' : 'symbol' : '1.2-2' : 'de' }}
Nursing Care Insurance (Pflegeversicherung)Funds long-term care for the elderly and disabled. The base rate is 3.6% (1.8% employee share). Childless employees over 23 pay an extra 0.6% surcharge (0.3% employee share). Parents get a 0.25% discount per child, up to 5. {{ result().nursingCareInsurance | currency: 'EUR' : 'symbol' : '1.2-2' : 'de' }}
